What We Offer

As Digital Health Librarians, we offer various services to support your digital health journey:

  • Curated Health Information: We provide reliable, evidence-based health resources.
  • Research Support: Helping users locate and understand health-related data & studies.
  • Digital Literacy Training: Teaching users how to identify trustworthy online health sources.
  • Personalized Health Guidance: Offering tailored support for your health needs.
  • Tech Support: Assisting users with mobile health apps and wearables.
  • Community Outreach: Promoting awareness of digital health tools and access.
